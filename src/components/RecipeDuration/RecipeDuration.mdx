# Recipe Duration

Displays a recipe's cook time alongside a clock icon.

## Guidelines

- Pass `duration` in minutes.
- Durations over 60 minutes are automatically formatted as hours and minutes (e.g. `1h 15min`).
- A `duration` of `0` or less displays `"n/a"` to indicate the time is unavailable.
