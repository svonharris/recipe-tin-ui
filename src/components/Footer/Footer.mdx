# Footer

A layout container that sits at the bottom of a page. It renders a semantic `<footer>` element with a top border and accepts any content via `children`.

## Guidelines

- Place the Footer at the bottom of the page, outside of any scrollable content areas.
- Use `children` to compose footer content — copyright notices, links, or secondary navigation.
- Keep footer content minimal and secondary in importance to the main page content.
