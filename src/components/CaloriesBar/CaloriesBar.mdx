# Calories Bar

Displays a segmented bar showing each macronutrient's caloric contribution as a proportion of the total.

## Guidelines

- Use CaloriesBar to visualise the caloric breakdown of all three macronutrients together.
- The `protein`, `carb`, and `fat` props are amounts in grams. Calories are calculated internally (`protein`/`carb` × 4, `fat` × 9).
- Each segment fills proportionally to its share of the total calculated calories.
- If a macronutrient value is zero, its segment is omitted and the remaining macros fill the bar.
- The calorie total displayed may differ slightly from API-provided values as it is derived from the 4-4-9 formula.
- Intended to be used alongside `NutrientBar` components for a full macronutrient breakdown.
