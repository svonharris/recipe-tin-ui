# Nutrient Bar

Displays the amount of a macronutrient as a labelled progress bar.

## Guidelines

- Use NutrientBar to visualise a single macronutrient value at a time.
- The `macronutrient` prop accepts `"protein"`, `"carb"`, or `"fat"` — each renders with a distinct colour.
- Unrecognised macronutrient values fall back to a neutral grey colour scheme.
- The `value` prop represents the amount in grams and is displayed as a label alongside the bar.
