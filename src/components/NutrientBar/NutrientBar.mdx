# Nutrient Bar

Displays a macronutrient's caloric contribution as a proportion of the recipe's total calories.

## Guidelines

- Use NutrientBar to visualise a single macronutrient's caloric contribution at a time.
- The `macronutrient` prop accepts `"protein"`, `"carb"`, or `"fat"` — each renders with a distinct colour.
- Unrecognised macronutrient values fall back to a neutral grey colour scheme.
- The `value` prop is the amount in grams. The component converts this to calories internally (`protein`/`carb` × 4, `fat` × 9).
- The `totalCalories` prop is the total calories of the recipe. The bar fills proportionally to the macronutrient's caloric share.
