# Ratings

Displays a star rating alongside a review count.

## Guidelines

- Pass a `rating` between `0` and `5`. Values outside this range are clamped automatically.
- The rating is rounded to the nearest whole number to determine how many stars are filled.
- Use `reviews` to show the total number of reviews alongside the numeric rating.
