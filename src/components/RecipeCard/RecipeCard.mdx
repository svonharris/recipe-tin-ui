# Recipe Card

Displays a summary of a recipe including its image, title, cook time, and ratings.

## Guidelines

- Provide a descriptive `title` so the user can immediately identify the recipe.
- Use `cookTime` to show the total cook time in minutes.
- `ratings` and `reviews` are optional — they default to `0` when not provided.
- Pair RecipeCard with a consistent image aspect ratio to keep card layouts uniform.
